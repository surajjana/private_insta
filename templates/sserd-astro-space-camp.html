Welcome to the Astro Space Camp 2017 where young aspiring ‘space explorers’ can discover the realms of deep space and developments in aerospace technologies. Society for Space Education Research and Development (SSERD) brings a thrilling 5-day exploration opportunity for 7 to 17-years-old filled with exciting fun and knowledge. Step into the world of space with SSERD and move one step closer to the stars with us.
